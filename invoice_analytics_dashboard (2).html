Insights & recommendations
1
Automate payment reminders at day 7, 14, and 30
55% of overdue invoices fall in the 0–30 day bucket — a simple automated nudge sequence before the due date and on D+7 can recover a large share of these before they age further. Clients like Skyline Media (22 days avg) are likely to respond to early reminders.
2
Introduce early-payment incentives for high-value clients
Your avg payment time is 19 days, but at-risk clients (Nexus, Horizon) are stretching past 40–68 days. Offering a 1–2% discount for payment within 10 days can reduce DSO and convert slow payers. Target this selectively at clients responsible for the top 60% of outstanding value.
3
Add credit limits and upfront deposits for critical accounts
Nexus Retail and Horizon Tech together account for ₹1,77,500 of overdue exposure. Require a 30–50% advance for new work with these accounts, and cap total outstanding credit at 1.5× their average monthly billing. This reduces write-off risk without ending the business relationship.
